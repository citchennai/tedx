COUNTDOWN

Countdown is a global initiative to educate the public about adverse effects of climate change and the need to protect the earth, to build a better future by cutting greenhouse gas emissions in half by 2030 in the race to the zero-carbon world - the world that is safer, cleaner and fairer for everyone. It is a movement open to everyone - and everyone has a vital role to play.
