We are thrilled to be back with another life-changing TEDx event, combining the power of the three forums. The Chennai Institute of Technology is pleased to inform you of TEDxChennaiInstituteOfTechnologyWomen.It is an endeavor to promote women in every corner of the world, and we are delighted to be a part of it.
We are returning to host TEDxChennaiInstituteOfTechnologyWomen after successful iterations of many TEDx events and 50+ presentations with worldwide reach and influence, as well as an exuberance of inventions and solutions.
This event will be held offline, with speakers from many walks of life addressing an even larger audience base, encompassing the whole spectrum of society, and creating a picture of the contributions and improvements that a group of empowered women can bring.

About TED

TED is a global community that welcomes individuals from all disciplines and cultures who want to gain a better knowledge of the world, and who believe strongly in the power of ideas to alter attitudes, lives, and, eventually, the world. Along with two annual conferences – the TED Conference in Long Beach, California each spring, and TEDGlobal in Edinburgh, UK each summer – TED includes the award-winning TED Talks site, which provides over 1,400 of the best talks and performances from TED and partners available to the world, for free, with more added each week.

About TED Women

TED Women, a global-driven movement, is all about women power. It opens up exciting opportunities for change-makers, creators aspiring to light a fire of passion with flickering ideas that brightens our future. TED Women brings together people and encourages them to pursue their dreams. The power of women is a force to be reckoned with. TED Women are there to make sure women's voices are heeded, not overlooked and ignored.

OUR MISSION

“Empowered Women Empower the World”

We hope to make certain that extraordinary voices are heard throughout the land. Women Power isn't about how women gained power. Women are already strong, it's about changing the way the world perceives that strength. History has always had a bright side of strong, independent, brave and intelligent women, despite some depressing periods and practices that are popularly proclaimed.

“We can save ourselves but only if we let go of the myth of dominance and mastery and learn to work with nature” - Naomi Klien

“We cannot despair of humanity , since we ourselves are human beings” Albert Einstein

As we dream and aim to build a better future, we would like to take the first step, use and educate them about what would happen if this continues. To accomplish this, we have great minds and youths from various backgrounds who have understood the hazardous changes occurring and have already begun their voyage to renew the world to its glory.

OUR VISION

We want to live in a society where everyone (and we mean everyone) is treated with dignity, has control over their life, and realizes their full potential. This is what we want for every woman, man, adult, kid, black, Asian, Latino, indigenous, white, gay, lesbian, transgender, straight, poor, rich, disabled, able-bodied, immigrant, refugee, citizen, and every other group of people on the earth.

Aim of the program - what impact do you want to bring ?

Bridging the gap between people knowing the facts about the adverse effects of climate change and actually understanding its impact is key to creating a better future.Protecting the earth and environment has always been an open ground, a movement open to everyone - and everyone has a vital role to play.

EVENT ORGANIZERS
